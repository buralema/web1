Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Ethiopian cultural clothing?

Ethiopian cultural clothing includes traditional garments worn by
various ethnic groups in Ethiopia. These garments often feature
intricate embroidery and are made from natural fabrics.

How can I purchase your products?

You can browse our products online and place an order through our website. We offer worldwide shipping.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and direct bank transfers.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes, we have a return and exchange policy. Please refer to our
return policy page for more details.

Do you offer custom-made clothing?

Yes, we offer custom-made clothing. Please contact us with your
requirements, and we will be happy to assist you.

Size

How can I find my correct size? We offer the majority of our
clothing items in a versatile free-size range, encompassing small,
medium, large, extra-large, and more. To ensure a perfect fit, you
have the option to refer to our comprehensive size guide for
accurate measurements. Alternatively, if you prefer, you can visit a
skilled tailor to obtain your measurements and subsequently share
them with us. Your satisfaction with the fit of our clothing is our
priority, and we are here to accommodate your preferences.
